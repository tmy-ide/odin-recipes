Filipino Spaghetti

Description

The Filipino Spaghetti is a funky take on the ever classic Italian spaghetti. While traditional spaghetti is tangy and savory, Filipino spaghetti is known to be sweet and savory (a common flavor palette for many Filipno dishes!). This dish is simple and also something that every kid can enjoy!

Instead of meatballs, ground beef is traditionally mixed into the sauce as well as pork sausage and even hotdog. Upon serving, cheddar cheese is springkled right on top!

Although, technically, not a Filipino dish, its recipe strays from the original which exemplifies the countless other Filipino dishes that are influenced by other cultures.
